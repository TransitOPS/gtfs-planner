Rider Narrative

You arrive at Grand Central by bus at 08:15. Enter through Main Entrance (marked "A"), which is wheelchair accessible. The entrance faces east.

Inside, follow signs toward Platforms 10–15. This is a 150-meter walk northeast through the main hall—the longest single segment of your station walk.

Take the stairs up to the Mezzanine level. At the top, tap your card at the fare gate and continue north.

Take the elevator down to the Platform Level (two levels below the Mezzanine). When you exit, follow signs east 85 meters to Platform 12.

Cross the platform to your boarding position. Your train departs at 08:25—you have approximately 2 minutes 45 seconds of margin.

Analytical note: The up-then-down vertical path (Ground → Mezzanine → Platform Level) adds complexity. Riders unfamiliar with the station may find this counterintuitive. The fare gate placement on the Mezzanine level forces this routing.
